Explanation..

This goal outlines the core mission of the company, which is to ensure that clean and safe drinking water is readily available to every individual. The approach is through the use of the Aquadropin app, which acts as a platform for ordering and scheduling the delivery of 25-liter water bottles.

Key components of the goal include:

Accessibility: The company aims to make clean drinking water accessible to everyone, emphasizing inclusivity and affordability.

Convenience: By using the Aquadropin app, customers can easily place orders and schedule deliveries, making the process of obtaining water hassle-free.

Reliability: The goal emphasizes the company's commitment to providing a dependable water delivery service, ensuring that customers can rely on them for their drinking water needs.

Sustainability: While not explicitly stated, the goal can include a commitment to environmentally friendly practices in the water delivery process, such as reusable or recyclable containers and eco-friendly delivery methods.
